Privacy Policy — Noor (v1)
Effective date: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Noor (the "App" or "we") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use the mobile application distributed through the Google Play Store.

1. Information we collect
- Information you provide: if the App offers any optional contact, feedback, or support forms you may supply your name, email address, or other information you choose to provide.
- Device & usage data: we automatically collect technical data about your device and how you use the App (device model, OS version, app version, crash reports, logs, and usage patterns).
- Location data: the App can request your device location for features such as Qibla direction or nearby mosque lookups. Location is only collected if you grant permission.
- Push notification tokens: we use push notifications (Firebase Cloud Messaging). Tokens are stored to enable delivery of notifications.
- Local storage: the App stores preferences and app data locally (for example using `shared_preferences` and local files).

2. How we use information
- Provide and maintain the App and its features.
- Personalize and improve the App experience and perform analytics to understand usage and improve functionality.
- Deliver push notifications and other messages if you opt in.
- Diagnose problems, fix bugs, and monitor performance and security.

3. Third-party services and SDKs
We use third-party services to operate the App. These may include (but are not limited to):
- Firebase (for push messaging, crash reporting, analytics, and cloud services).
- Google services used by the Play Store.
These providers may collect and process information according to their own privacy policies. You should review their policies for details.

4. Sharing and disclosure
We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:
- Service providers and subprocessors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, crash reporting, push messaging).
- When required by law, or to protect rights, property or safety.

5. Data retention
We retain data as long as necessary to provide the App and for legitimate business purposes (e.g., troubleshooting or compliance). You may request deletion via the contact details below.

6. Security
We use commercially reasonable technical and organizational measures to help protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

7. Your choices
- Location & notifications: you can disable location permissions or notifications in your device settings; disabling may affect app features.
- Delete data: contact us (see below) to request access, correction, or deletion of personal data.

8. Children's privacy
The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us to request deletion.

9. International transfers
Data may be processed in countries other than your home country. When we transfer data internationally, we take steps to protect it as required by applicable law.

10. Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the revised policy and update the effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact
